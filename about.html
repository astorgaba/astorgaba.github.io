Miguel Astorga Badia

Games, C++ and graphics programmer

I'm a 21-year-old Spanish guy with a passion for video game programming, graphics, and low-level development. Currently, I find myself deeply immersed in the world of coding, with a particular focus on C++ and low level and graphics development.

I started off learning C++ as my first programming language at ESAT Valencia. After a year and a half of learning to code, I started to develop using OpenGL as my first graphics API and Unreal Engine 4.

I realised that I was really interested in graphics programming, and I started to investigate and develop in other lower lever graphics APIs like DX12 and Vulkan, alongside the versatile WebGPU. That took me to my fourth year programming, where I find myself coding in the PS5 low level API.

On the other hand, I also enjoyed a lot creating things in Unreal Engine. I had the opportunity to work with 6 other programmers, 6 artists and 3 game designers to create an amazing videogame that got to be released in STEAM: "ANDARA: Rise for Rebellion".

In essence, I am a dedicated and versatile video game programmer, weaving together a tapestry of skills in C++, Unreal Engine 4, and a diverse array of programming languages and graphics APIs. Always eager to learn and grow, I am on a perpetual quest to push the boundaries of what is possible in the ever-evolving landscape of game development.
