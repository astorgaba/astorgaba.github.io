SpaceShock

Project Date Jan. 2022 - May. 2023
Role Designer, Gameplay programmer

In this project, I worked along another classmate, Mario Galan. We were given a template for this project, which let us create enemies and their basic behaviors. Then, we had to create the whole aesthetic and further gameplay things.

My main role in this project was to create the enemies AI and the enemies wave spawning system along 2 other coding mates. The enemies that I put the most work on were the Spawner and both Ranged Special enemies (flying and regular). Other than AI, I also programmed the functionality for some gameplay stuff like moving platforms, doors, triggers, ragdoll and enemies animation, between some things.
